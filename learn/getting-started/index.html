Getting started

Getting started with biicode is a quick way to develop your projects, able to work in different conditions and environments.

$ bii init -L a new project.

Search for blocks (above) and #include “username/blockname/lib/library.h”

$ bii find retrieves the code to your project and lists the dependencies in biicode.conf text file.

Fill your specs in your biicode.conf and $ bii build

$bii publish or $bii publish -r to share. Just one command.
