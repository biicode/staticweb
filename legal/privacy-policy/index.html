Biicode agrees to process the personal data provided by its users under absolute confidentiality.

By navigating through the Website, the Users provide Biicode information about their IP address (unique number used to transmit packages between devices connected to the Internet), location, date and hour of access, time spent on the Website, kind of device, operating system or browser used. In any event this information of access cannot be communicated or disclosed to any third party without your previous authorization or unless we receive a request from a competent authority, in compliance with the applicable law.

Access data to the services will be stored and used with the sole purpose of supervising the service and carrying out statistics about the access of the service. In addition, the access data will be used to avoid the access to the Website or the use of the App by users who access the services through an IP that may have previously infringed the terms of our Terms and Conditions or the Community Guidelines.
4. USE OF COOKIES

In fulfilment of the provisions set forth in article 22 of the Spanish E-Commerce Act, we inform you that the Website uses or may use in the future its own cookies and third party cookies for the analysis of use of the Website and to provide the service as useful as possible. Cookies are small text files that are sent to your browser and make it possible to remember information of your previous sessions, but in any case cookies are not meant for your identification.

Cookies may be used to personalize and improve the Users’ experience, facilitate the use of the services, and for statistical and analytical purposes. You are able to, if you want to, configure your browser to reject the installation of these files and, if so, to remove them in case they are already installed. If you continue browsing, we will consider that you accept their use.

For further information about cookies you can visit HTTP_cookie in Wikipedia.
Biicode uses Google Analytics, which is a service provided by Google Inc., that can install cookies in your device. For further information about the features of this service, please visit this website, where Google Inc., makes available useful information, so that if you wish you can disable this service. For further technical information about this service, you can visit this site.

Biicode may disclose or communicate to a third party in a future aggregated and non-personal data in order to show global trends and statistics for the general use of its services.

You may remove your user account at any time. This removal implies the erasure of the personal data that you may have provided to Biicode, except those data necessary for the proper functioning of Biicode, in particular the data necessary for the compliance and enforcement of the agreements that you have signed through the platform.
The removal of your personal data when your account is disabled may not be immediate.
